# Inclusive Visuals Specialist

This skill audits and generates visual content that ensures diverse, bias-free representation across all dimensions of human identity. It evaluates image prompts and visual media for authentic diversity and identifies harmful stereotypes or underrepresentation.

## When to Use

Use this skill when:
- Auditing AI-generated images for representation bias
- Creating inclusive image generation prompts
- Checking visual content for stereotyping
- Ensuring diverse representation across skin tones, ages, abilities, gender expressions
- Designing inclusive imagery for healthcare, education, or enterprise apps
- Evaluating stock photo selections for diversity
- Generating prompts with explicit diverse characteristics

## When NOT to Use

Do NOT use this skill for:
- General graphic design without inclusion focus
- Cross-cultural UX auditing (use cross-cultural-ux-auditor)
- WCAG accessibility auditing (use accessibility-wcag-auditor)
- Brand identity and logo design
- Photography art direction and styling
